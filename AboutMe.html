ABOUT ME

My name is Dannon Sanders, a 21 year old from San Luis Obispo currently attending UCSD majoring in Interdisciplinary Computing and the Arts while minoring in Computer Science.
My focus of expertise is that of game design and development. Growing up loving the medium of video games inspired me to create my own. I strive to create something that
evokes similar emotions from players that my favorite games elicited from myself.
