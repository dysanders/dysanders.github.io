VIS 160A: Senior Project TEST

I will document my progess and eventual finished product for my senior project, a game entirely developed by myself entitled A Mixed Bag.
It resembles an exploration and turn based RPG style approach to gameplay. The player will have to search for and utilize a myriad of varying items in different encounters in order to traverse through a new environment.
The catch, as the player will soon come to learn, is that each item does not behave as expected. A brief label or description will accompany each found object; however, upon use the collectible's action will instead depict a witty pun or play on words.
It can range from a literalist interpretation, a simple joke or an ironic homonym - anything to garner a chuckle from the player as they realize what the item's function actually is. A Mixed Bag will benefit from the player's previous experience from games as it will turn common video game and everyday items on their head in unexpected and pleasantly foolish ways.
